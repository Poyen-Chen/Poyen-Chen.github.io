About Me

Who I Am

I'm an AI Research Engineer at the Industrial Technology Research Institute (ITRI), with an M.Sc. in Simulation Sciences from RWTH Aachen and a B.Sc. in Chemical Engineering from NCKU. My work sits at the intersection of AI and physics: building PINN and operator-learning surrogate models that accelerate dissipative particle dynamics (DPD) and CFD simulations for microfluidic design and particle-size prediction, alongside production-grade agentic RAG systems and LLM-driven knowledge bases.

I enjoy being the bridge between scientists and scalable software — turning research-grade code into maintainable ML systems, covering the full lifecycle from data pipelines and experiment tracking to deployment (Docker, Terraform, Airflow, MLflow, CI/CD).
