Welcome to My Portfolio

I'm an AI Research Engineer at the Industrial Technology Research Institute (ITRI), with an M.Sc. in Simulation Sciences from RWTH Aachen. I work at the intersection of AI and physics: accelerating molecular dynamics and CFD simulations with physics-informed neural networks (PINNs) and operator learning as surrogate models for microfluidic design, while also building production-grade agentic RAG and LLM systems. I turn research-grade code into maintainable, scalable ML products, backed by a full MLOps toolchain (Docker, Terraform, Airflow, MLflow, CI/CD).
