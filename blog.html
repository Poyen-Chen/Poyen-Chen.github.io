Supporting the Taiwanese Community: My Role as Web Maintainer for TWSV Aachen

I'm excited to share my involvement with the Taiwanese Student Association Aachen (TWSV Aachen) as their web maintainer since 2023. This role allows me to combine my technical skills with my passion for supporting the Taiwanese community in Germany.

As a Taiwanese student in Aachen, I value community and cultural connection. I'm proud to serve as the web maintainer for the Taiwanese Student Association Aachen e.V., contributing to this vibrant community that bridges Taiwanese students and the international community.

About TWSV Aachen

The Taiwanese Student Association Aachen (德國阿亨台灣同學會) promotes cultural exchange and friendship through various events including welcome parties, Taiwan Night celebrations, Christmas gatherings, and Lunar New Year celebrations.

My Role as Web Maintainer

As the web maintainer, I'm responsible for:

  • Maintaining and updating the official website
  • Managing content updates and event announcements

This role aligns with my full-stack development background and commitment to serving the community. It's rewarding to use my technical skills to help connect Taiwanese students and promote our culture in Aachen.
